My Top 3 Favorite Movies Of All Time

Five Feet Apart

This one made me fall in love with love, eventhough I am single. I really liked the way of how love is portrayed between two young but terminally ill humans. If you love some close to heart feel good love, this movie is definetly worth watching. I promise you won't regret watching.

Violet Evergarden

This movie and series in Netflix will just make you feel grateful for everything you have in your life. It shows how the lead character finds her purpose of life after serving in the war and the losing the only person she loved in her life.

KGF

It is a blockbuster movie where you will experience goosebumbs thoughout the movie. Each and every scene in the movie will make you say "WOW". Especially the way the director protrayed the mother sentiment and son's love towards his mother is just awesome. Just watch it, you will forget yourself during the movie.
